Air Fryer Pork Chops

Boneless pork chops cooked to perfection.

Use your trusty air fryer to make the best (and easiest) pork chops of your life. Trust us - once you try this air fryer pork chop recipe, you'll never go back to your old method again.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan chesse
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 (5 once) boneless pork chops
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 380 degrees F
  2. Combine Parmesan cheese, paprika, garlic powder, salt, parsley, and pepper in a flat shallow dish; mix well.
  3. Coat each pork chop with olive oil. Dredge both sides of each chop in the Parmesan mixture and set on a plate.
  4. Place 2 chops in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 10 min; flipping halfway through cook time. Transfer to a cutting board and let rest for 5 min. Repeat with remaining chops.
  5. Served hot and enjoy!
